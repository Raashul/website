Fascinating book. There is a real mystery on how 1 guy can run 3+ multi-billion company at once. This book dives into Elon's history, his personality and his deep desire to win. A close look into how he prioterizes his day, runs meetings and what he expects from people who work for him.
One of the key takeaway from him is how is absolutely rejects mediocrity, and not shy to piss people off.

I found this book to be better than the other Elon by Ashlee Vance. Overall, a must-read book for anyone who wants to start a company.
