The book talks about the origin and history of the main key players in AI; [Sam Altman](https://x.com/sama) (OpenAI), [Demis Hassabis](https://x.com/demishassabis) (Deepmind), [Dario Amodei](https://x.com/DarioAmodei) (Anthropic), [Ilya Sutskever](https://x.com/ilyasut) (OpenAI co-founder), [Elon Musk](https://x.com/elonmusk) (xAI), [Satya Nadella](https://x.com/satyanadella) (Microsoft). Overall, for someone who is looking to get
some knowledge on the origin of how the biggest AI companies came to be, this book will definately give an broad perspective.
