Building a your personal AI Doctor(s) by integrating your favorite LLMs. Analyze your and your family reports, chat

You upload medical reports and multiple AI doctors will analye them and give you a diverse medical perspective in plain language. It maintains a historic context of you and
your family member.

Chat with your doctor directly in whatsapp or telegram anytime you have questions. Your AI doctor will always remember you and your family medical history
