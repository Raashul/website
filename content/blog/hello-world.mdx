This is the beginning. I've been meaning to carve out a space online that feels like mine — not a LinkedIn profile, not a Twitter thread, just a place to think out loud.

## Why this exists

I've always found that writing helps me think. Not polished, edited, publish-ready writing — just the act of putting thoughts into words. This blog is that.

## What to expect

Mostly technical writing, some product thinking, occasional tangents. If something's on my mind, it'll probably end up here.
